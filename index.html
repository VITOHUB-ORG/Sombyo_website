About Us

We Help Our Clients
To Grow Their Business

At SOMBYO Financial Services Company Limited, we are dedicated to empowering individuals and small businesses to achieve financial independence and long-term success.

We do this by providing a comprehensive range of financial services, with a core focus on microfinance, savings, and tailored loan solutions. Our services are designed to meet the unique needs of entrepreneurs, traders, and underserved communities.

Through personalized support, financial education, and responsible lending, we aim to create lasting impact and foster economic growth across the regions we serve.

Peter J. Mwakyusa and his wife, Mary B. Dittu, are the founders of Sombyo Financial Services Company Ltd, a company established with the goal of providing microloan services to entrepreneurs, individuals, and public servants in Tanzania.

The idea of starting this company began as a long-held dream for Peter, dating back about thirteen years. Through hard work and determination, that dream finally became a reality in 2025 when Sombyo Financial Services Company Ltd was officially registered and obtained all the necessary licenses to operate in accordance with the law.

Peter J. Mwakyusa holds an Advanced Diploma in Accountancy from Tanzania Institute of Accountancy (TIA) in 2010. He also earned a Master Degree of Science in Finance and Investment (MSc – FI) from the Institute of Accountancy Arusha (IAA) in 2024—a qualification that has played a significant role in the founding and management of the company.

To provide accessible financial services, with a focus on microfinance, to empower individuals, micro and small enterprises, and communities in Tanzania, fostering sustainable economic growth and development.

We achieve this by offering a range of services including microfinance loans, mobile banking, micro insurance, and financial training, tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients.

We are committed to operating with efficiency, and a deep understanding of the local context to ensure that our services contribute financial inclusion.

To be the leading and most trusted financial partner in Tanzania, driving sustainable economic empowerment and prosperity through innovative and inclusive financial solutions.

Our vision is to create a future where every individual and business has the opportunity to achieve their full potential through access to reliable and transformative financial services.
